# Code of Conduct

## 1. Purpose
`goswift` is committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment.

## 2. Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and constructive
- Assume good intentions
- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Focus on collaboration

## 3. Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment
- Discrimination
- Personal attacks
- Trolling or insults
- Publishing private information

## 4. Reporting
If you encounter behavior violating this code, please open a confidential issue or contact:

**maintainer: jawadalik91 (GitHub)**

## 5. Enforcement
- Violations may result in warnings or bans
- Severe or repeated violations will not be tolerated
